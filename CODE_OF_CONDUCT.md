# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **EduWaka**, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and graciously accepting constructive feedback.
- Taking responsibility and apologising when we make mistakes, and learning from the experience.
- Showing empathy and kindness toward other community members.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the mission of EduWaka.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment or discrimination in any form.
- Trolling, insulting, or making personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information (e.g. email address or location) without their explicit permission.
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwelcome advances of any kind.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainer **Gift Egbonyi** is responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. In response to behaviour deemed harmful, inappropriate, or in violation of this Code of Conduct, the maintainer reserves the right to take action including — but not limited to — issuing a warning, removing a comment or contribution, temporarily banning, or permanently removing a contributor from the project.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and the project repository. It also applies when an individual is representing EduWaka in public spaces — for example, posting on social media, speaking at an event, or communicating on behalf of the project.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour, please report it by emailing **Gift Egbonyi** at [egbonyigiftawase02@gmail.com](mailto:egbonyigiftawase02@gmail.com).

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with confidentiality. You can expect an acknowledgement within **72 hours** and a resolution or update within **7 days** where possible. The maintainer is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of anyone who reports an incident.

# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **EduWaka**, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism graciously
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others’ private information without permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainer **Gift Egbonyi** is responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. In cases of unacceptable behavior, the maintainer may take appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions) and in public spaces where a person represents the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting **Gift Egbonyi** by [email](egbonyigiftawase02@gmail.com). All complaints will be reviewed and addressed confidentially.
